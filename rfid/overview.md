## Summary

RFID (Radio-frequency identification)

- Electromagnetic fields to automatically identify and track tags attached to objects

| Type             | Frequency                            |
|------------------|--------------------------------------|
| Low (LF)         | 125-134 hz                           |
| High (HF)        | 13.56 Mhz                            |
| Ultra High (UHF) | 902-928 Mhz (US) or 865-868 Mhz (EU) |
